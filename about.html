About Me

I'm

Animating the narrative

When I was little, it was hard to get me out of the library. Books were doorways into other worlds; the text on a page would come to life in my mind's eye.

My career as a technical writer presented the perfect platform for telling an interactive story. I wrote about technical solutions and developed a user experience around that data to heighten understanding. This began in a basic way; by developing output templates for web and printed materials, I could use images, whitespace, and layouts to guide users through the information required.

When my focus moved to web applications, this opened a galaxy of potential for creating information products. By layering JavaScript, jQuery, and database technologies into a solution, the narrative moves from a monologue to a dialogue with users engaged in building the learning experiences they need.

I'm excited to build new interactive arenas for in-product communications.
